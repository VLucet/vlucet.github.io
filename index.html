About Me

Hi there! 👋 My name is Val and I write code and design tools to allow us to take better care of the environment.

I am an Ecologist at APEX RMS, and an   package developer (and aspiring Research software engineer) at Concordia Univeristy in Montreal (in the lab of Dr. Eric Pedersen). I am especially interested in reproducibility and open science. In my free time I love to bird at the Montreal Mont-Royal park. Originally from 🇫🇷 but from 🇨🇦 at heart.

I hold a B.Sc. (Hons) and a M.Sc. both in Ecology, from McGill University.
