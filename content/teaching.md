### [Git And Github With R and Rstudio](https://vlucet.github.io/git-and-github-with-r-workshop/)
**Level:** beginner  
**Length:** 1 hour (preparation) + 3 hours (material)  

A small workshop geared toward R users, made by collating different open source material and aimed at teaching the basics of the version control, with both the `git` command and Rstudio as a git interface.
